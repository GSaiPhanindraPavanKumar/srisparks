SRI SPARKS INFRA PRIVATE LIMITED

SriSparks Infra Pvt Ltd is a vibrant startup in India promoted by Singapore's M/s Rishi Engineering PTE Ltd. We offer innovative and secure infrastructure solutions, delivering unmatched reliability, scalability, and sustainability.

At Sri Sparks Infra, our goal is to transform the infrastructure market by offering all-inclusive EPC and O&M services. Our superior, effective, and sustainable infrastructure solutions are achieved via the application of modern technology and innovative methodologies.

Our Singapore-based partner, Rishi Engineering, has been providing engineering contracting services since 2016, with a strong background in project management and execution.
